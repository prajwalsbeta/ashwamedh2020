About us

Since its inception in 2010, Ashwamedh has been a high point of AISSMS College of Engineering, Pune. It is well known that for the overall growth of an individual, academic performance should be corroborated by extra-curricular participation and skill development. The motive behind Ashwamedh was to be an event where unification of the cultural and sports events could be found under one roof for students. In the past 9 years, the event has been waxing. This year we bring to you Ashwamedh 2020, the biggest fest you might have ever seen. The event will span for 3 days and is the ideal platform for students to underscore their skills in chess, box-cricket, 5-a-side football, kabaddi, volleyball, basketball, poetry, DJ War,street play,mobile gamming, sur-sargam and hit-the-floor. The prime attractions of this year’s fest are DJ war and Pubg. The event provides a stage for showcasing the cultural skills and a zone for locking horns on the basis of their sports prowess. So, as we enter our 10th year with the participation growing every year, we know this is a never-ending process and we aim to make this event catchy and it’s just a matter of time before this event becomes one of the biggest in our nation. - Team Ashwamedh
