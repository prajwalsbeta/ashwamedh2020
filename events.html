This event consists of 1 round.

1) The time limit for the performance is 3 – 4 mins (+1 preparation time).

2)  Use of properties/props is allowed.

4) The music tracks used for dance routines should be submitted to the organizers  in a labelled CD /pen drive.The tracks should be strictly in MP3 format.

5) The list of props or anything unusual (based on discretion of the organizing team) to be used should be submitted to the Organizers (Events Head) .

6)  In-case of malfunctioning, the participants will be allowed to perform  from the beginning or from the point where the performance stopped with the consent of the judges as well as the Organizers.

7) Participants exceeding the time limit will be negatively marked.

8) In case of any conflicts the decision of management and Judges will be final.

9) Participants need to carry their Identity card.

10)  Participants must be present before two hour before starting the event.

11) Participants must do registration before two hour before starting event

SUR SARGAM




Sur Sargam (Solo Singing)

1. There will be two rounds in the competition:

Elimination: Participants must sing for a minimum of 1 minute and a

maximum of 3 minutes.

Final Round: The participants must sing for a minimum of 2 minutes and

maximum of 5 minutes.

NOTE: Karaoke will be allowed only for the final round. Best of First round will be selected for Final round

2. Participants will have to perform according to the number given to them

at the time of reporting. In case the participant misses his/her turn, the

participant will be accommodated later only if time permits.

3. The sound track of the karaoke must be submitted to the organizing

team in a CD/Pen Drive. Back up of the same should be carried by the

participant at the time of performance.

4. Only one personal instrument is allowed. The participant himself/herself

is allowed to play it.

5. Each participant will be given only one chance to perform, failing which

the participant will be disqualified.

6. Reporting time is 30 minutes before the scheduled time. Your slots will

not change in any condition.

7. Every participant should have his/her own valid college I-card and receipt

failing which, the respective participant will not be allowed to participate.(Entry Fess will not be refunded)

8. Decision of judges and the organizing committee will be final

PEN IT DOWN




Pen it down Rule Book :

  • The event consists of only one round.
  • If plagiarism is found then the participant will be disqualified.
  • Languages: Hindi, Marathi, English.

Primary Round

  1. Contestants must prepare a content on any of the provided topics on the spot.
  2. Poetry of any sort will not be considered as content in this competition. 
  3. If any content of a notable writer is used in the writeup, proper references are to be mentioned or else it will be considered as plagiarism.
  4. Contestants must finish the write up within 60 minutes. No extra time will be provided.
  5. Grading will be done by the judges and the winners will be selected. There will not be a second round.

PUBG




BATTLEZONE


Event Name: BattleZone – PUBG MOBILE
Fees: Rs.200 per Group
Participation Type: Group of 4 people

Event Rules

1. The Tournament is comprised entirely of squads. Players must combine to form a squad consisting of four (4) Players.

2. Room ID and Password shared to the team leader.

3. Players should join in-game Lobby without any delay once they receive Room Credentials.

4. All matches played in TPP Erangle map.

5. Participants are not allowed to share the password on to anyone.

6. No cheating (USE OF EMULATORS, AIMBOTS, WALL HACKS, SPEEDHACKS, Use of a modified/jail broken console, in game hacks, and/or mods ETC.) All players will be closely monitored and if anyone is caught cheating, that player will be disqualified and banned from the server too

7. Players are requested to save the end game screenshot of their standings and kills. once a game ends and must provide it to the administrators if asked.

8. In the event of a disconnection, the Player that is disconnected will not be able to re-join the Match. Should the last remaining Player on a Team disconnect, that Team will be granted the finish position that their last characters die at.

9. In the event of a full server crash, Tournament Organizers will issue a replay of that particular Game, at their sole discretion.

10. Tournament Organizers may halt a Game or Match at any time and for specific technical reason. In the event of a stoppage of play, Players must remain at their devices and may not communicate with other Players until the Game or Match is resumed.

11. The Tournament Organizers may order a Game or Match restart due to exceptional circumstances, such as if a bug significantly affects a Player’s ability to play or the Game or Match is disrupted by a Force Majeure or other event.

12. All Participants will receive participation certificate.

*Any Player behaving inappropriately, or not competing in compliance with these Official Rules as determined by the Tournament Organizers in their sole discretion, may be immediately disqualified from the Tournament and forfeit all potential prizes.
